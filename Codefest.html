The 6-Step Collaboration Journey

LEAD: SCHOOL

1. Initial Planning & Event Design

The school sets the foundation: defining the theme, categories, grade-level mapping, and duration of the competition.

LEAD: QUBITS

2. Challenge & Task Ideation

Based on the school's plan, Qubits proposes 2-4 engaging and creative challenge ideas for each category.

LEAD: SCHOOL

3. Final Challenge Selection

The school reviews the ideas and selects the one best-suited challenge for each category to move forward with.

LEAD: QUBITS

4. Rubric Development

For each selected challenge, Qubits creates a comprehensive, clear, and fair evaluation rubric.

LEAD: SCHOOL

5. Evaluation & Finalist Selection

The school conducts the initial evaluation for all entries using the Qubits rubric and shortlists the top finalists.

LEAD: COLLABORATIVE

6. Final Evaluation & Recognition

Qubits can assist in final judging, but the school makes the final winner decision. We then co-brand and provide certificates for the winners.
